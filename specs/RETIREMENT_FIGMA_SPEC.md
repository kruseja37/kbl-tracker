# Retirement System UI/UX Design Specification

> **For**: Figma Design Team
> **App**: KBL XHD Tracker (iPad-first baseball league management)
> **Feature**: Offseason Phase 5 - Retirements
> **Created**: January 29, 2026

---

## Overview

The Retirement phase is a **team-by-team ceremony** where players retire based on age-weighted dice rolls. The goal is **1-2 retirements per team per season**. The UI should feel like:
- **Awards night tension** (probability reveals, dice rolls)
- **Hall of fame ceremony** (jersey retirement, celebration)
- **Sports app clarity** (player cards, stats display)

### Design Principles
1. **Dramatic Reveals** - Build tension before showing who retires
2. **Celebratory Moments** - Jersey retirement should feel special
3. **Touch-First** - Large tap targets for iPad (44pt minimum)
4. **Baseball Aesthetic** - Team colors, jersey-style displays
