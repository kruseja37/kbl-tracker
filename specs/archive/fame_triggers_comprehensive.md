## Fame Entry Quick Reference

### Common In-Game Fame Moments

**Easy +1 Bonuses:**
- Walk-off hit
- Grand slam
- Robbing home run
- Complete game shutout
- 10+ K game
- Milestone achievement

**Easy -1 Boners:**
- 4+ strikeouts in game
- Error allowing run
- Blown save
- TOOTBLAN
- Nutshot

### Pre-Season Fame Assignments

At season start, assign Fame based on reputation:

| Player Type | Starting Fame |
|-------------|---------------|
| All-time legend (Ruth, Mays, etc.) | +3 |
| Hall of Famer | +2 |
| Star player (A- or better) | +1 |
| Fan favorite (user designated) | +1 |
| Notorious villain | +1 (or -1, your choice) |
| Known choker | -1 |
| Recent bust | -1 |

## Fame Decay (Optional Rule)

To prevent Fame from snowballing:
- At All-Star break: Fame over +5 reduces by 1
- At All-Star break: Fame below -5 increases by 1
- This keeps Fame relevant but not overwhelming

## Fame Impact Summary

Fame affects:
1. **All-Star Voting** (part of 20% Narrative component)
2. **MVP Voting** (part of 8% Narrative component)
3. **Cy Young Voting** (part of 5% Narrative component)
4. **ROY Voting** (part of Narrative component)
5. **Gold Glove "Eye Test"** (fielding Fame specifically)
6. **Kara Kawaguchi Award** (clutch/narrative heavy)
7. **Manager of the Year** (team Fame aggregated)

Fame does NOT affect:
- League Leader awards (purely statistical)
- Ratings calculations
- WAR calculations
- Clutch point values
