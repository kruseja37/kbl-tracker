## Design Principles

1. **Dramatic Reveals**: Awards should feel like real ceremonies with suspense
2. **User Agency**: Hybrid voting lets users override system recommendations
3. **Clear Information Hierarchy**: Stats and voting breakdowns easy to scan
4. **Sequential Flow**: Fixed order, can't skip ahead
5. **Celebration**: Winners should feel special with animations and emphasis

- Leaders auto-calculated from season stats
- Rewards shown inline next to qualifying leaders
- Ties show both players (e.g., "M. Trout / J. Soto (.342)")
- Rewards summary expandable at bottom
- Single "Continue" button to proceed

|--------|--------|

## Animation & Transition Notes

### Winner Reveal Animations
1. **Build-up**: 1-2 second suspense with dimmed background
2. **Reveal**: Photo slides up or zooms in from center
3. **Celebration**: Confetti or sparkle effect (brief)
4. **Stats Display**: Fade in after 0.5s delay

### Screen Transitions
- Slide left for "Continue" navigation
- Fade for modal overlays
- Quick snap for position indicator updates

### Performance Considerations
- Pre-load next screen data during current screen
- Cache player photos
- Limit animation complexity on older iPads
