> **Feature Area**: Offseason Phase 4 - Contraction/Expansion

## Design Principles

1. **High Stakes Drama**: Contraction is emotional - treat it with gravity
2. **Clear Consequences**: Show exactly what happens to players and teams
3. **User Control**: Voluntary sales and protection selection are meaningful choices
4. **Historical Weight**: Connect to museum/legacy systems
5. **Expansion Optimism**: New teams should feel exciting, not bureaucratic
